Audition Process


Stage 1: Online Video Submission

  • In the first stage, we ask you to showcase your musical talents by uploading a video of you performing a song or demonstrating your playing skills. This can be an individual performance where you show us your best abilities, whether you're a vocalist, instrumentalist, or composer. Make sure the video is no longer than 5 minutes.
  • Once we review your video submission, successful candidates will be invited to proceed to the second stage.

Stage 2: Live Audition

  • If you pass the first stage, you'll be invited to an in-person audition. During this stage, you will be required to perform a song of your choice live in front of the AITU Music Club panel. After your performance, we will conduct a short interview where we'll ask about your musical background, experiences, and motivations for joining our club.
  • This is your chance to not only impress us with your talent but also show your passion for music and how you would contribute to our community.
